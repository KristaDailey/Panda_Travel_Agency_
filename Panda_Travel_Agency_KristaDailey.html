Punaluu Black Sand Beach Hawaii

Welcome to the website of Punaluu Black Sand Beach Hawaii!

If you're looking for a unique new place to visit Punaluu Sand Beach In Hawaii may be the perfect destination for you! The black sand and rare animal sightings attract many visitors year round. This location is very family friendly and includes many fun activities.

Why Should You Go Here?

Punaluu Black Sand Beach is the most popular black sand beach in Hawaii. Not only is it a beautiful beach, but you can sight see endangered Sea Turtles! The Hawksbill turtles are an endangered species and frequently emerge onto the shore at this beach. This beach is very unique since Black Sand beaches are very rare. The beach is easily accessible and is near other attractions. In general, Hawaii is a very popular vacation spot for many families. Going here could be a once in a lifetime experience.

Activities Punaluu Beach Offers

Swimming:

You are allowed to swim in the ocean, but there are no security or lifeguards on duty. This means swim at your own risk. They advise to not go swimming when surf tides are high.In the water, they are underwater springs. This causes the ocean to be cold and hot at the same time.

Snorkeling

At Punaluu's Ninole cove you can go snorkeling! It' a short walk from the main beach. They provide restrooms and facilities as well.

Hiking & Camping

Hiking is free to everyone who visits the beach. The beach is very big and is great for hiking with friends or family. Camping on the other hand, you need a permit.
